## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters and hiring managers looking for internship/full-time candidates. Clients seeking collaborators for projects.

### Brand Personality
Minimal & focused — clean design that lets the work speak. Professional but approachable.

### Aesthetic Direction
Inspired by reference: jdhruv143.vercel.app — Minimalist, typography-driven, generous whitespace, subtle accents, clean grid layouts. Light theme default. No decorative flourishes.

### Design Principles
1. **Less is more** — Every element must earn its place
2. **Typography-first** — Let text hierarchy do the heavy lifting
3. **Functional minimalism** — No glassmorphism, gradients, or visual noise
4. **Consistent spacing** — Use a deliberate spacing scale throughout
5. **Purposeful interactions** — Hover states and transitions serve function, not decoration
